Pull ups
10x4
Assisted pull ups. Grab the bar at wider than shoulder width and keep elbows out. Look up while pulling.
Rows
12x3 (each side)
Dumbell rows. Perform on a straight bench. Keep back straight and parallel to the floor.
Read deltoids
10x4
Rear deltoids fly. Keep arms and back straight. Look up while pulling and keep elbows out.
Lower back
7x5
Lower back extensions. Keep back straight, do not over extend. Carry a small weight if too easy.
