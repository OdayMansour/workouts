Shoulder press
10x2 + 8 + 4
Machine shoulder press. Do not tense neck, keep back against the bench. Use full range of motion.
Shrugs
12x4
Dumbell shrugs. Do not tense neck, keep arm straight down. Maintain upwards position for 2 seconds.
Calf raises
12x4
Machine calf raises. Keep knees straight, maintain extended position for 2 seconds. Perform slowly and do not swing.
Chest fly
10x4
Chest fly machine. Keep arms straight and back to the bench. Keep shoulders straight up and do not twist them in.
